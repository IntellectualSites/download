Information:

IntellectualSites is the home of open source projects for and by the minecraft community. Our projects are available for a plenty of platforms, for example Bukkit, Sponge, Forge, NukkitX and others.
Alongside plugins and mods, we are developing lots of other open source projects as well. For example custom web frameworks, paste services and asset databases for our projects.
Team Members: Citymonstret, dordosor21, IronApollo, Jesse, MattBDev, NotMyFault and RedstoneFuture

Projects:

  • FastAsyncVoxelSniper (FAVS)
  • PlotSquared (P2)
