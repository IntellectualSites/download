What is the Paste Viewer?
The paste viewer is used to visualize our debug pastes in a modern and stylish way.

  • Supports JSON to read its content
  • Is fully interactive
  • Is (semi) mobile friendly
  • Allows our developers an in-depth look into your systems within a few seconds
  • Please note: Downloads are currently unavailable, but you can build it from source (click "Source" in the header)
